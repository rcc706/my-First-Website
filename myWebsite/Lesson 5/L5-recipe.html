Hotdogs

Here is my unique gourmet Hotdog recipe.

Ingredients

  • Hotdogs
  • Hotdog buns
  • Mustard
  • Relish
  • Chopped onions

Directions

  1. Preheat grill to 350°.
  2. Place dogs on grill & roll occasionally for even cooking.
  3. Place cooked hotdogs in buns.
  4. Apply mustard, relish, and onions to taste.
  5. Enjoy your hotdogs!
